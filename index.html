Abstract

We introduce DefectZoo, a large-scale and diverse dataset of real-world industrial defects designed to support benchmarking of visual inspection models. The dataset spans multiple domains including textiles, metallic surfaces, printed circuit boards (PCBs), and soft displays. Each image is annotated with bounding boxes, segmentation masks, or image-level labels to support various learning paradigms including classification, object detection, and semantic segmentation. By consolidating heterogeneous sources and defect types into a single structured benchmark, DefectZoo enables systematic comparison of algorithmic generalization and cross-domain robustness.
